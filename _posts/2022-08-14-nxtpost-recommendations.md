NxtPost is a graph-based recommendation framework designed for Facebook Groups to help users discover relevant content within their communities.

### Abstract:
Recommending relevant posts to users within millions of Facebook Groups presents significant scale and cold-start challenges. This paper details the architecture and deployment of NxtPost, including how we handle high-velocity content and heterogeneous user interests.

*Presented at the **2022 ACM SIGKDD (KDD)** conference.*
